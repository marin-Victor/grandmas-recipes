Hamburger Pie

Ingredients

  • ground beef 1 pound
  • Potato Buds instant puffs (enough for 8 servings)/em>
  • egg 1
  • salt 1 teaspoon
  • pepper 1/8 teaspoon
  • instant minced onion 1 tablespoon
  • catsup 3/4 cup
  • shredded sharp Cheddar cheese 1/2 cup
  • milk 1 cup

Preparation

  1. Heat oven to 350°.

    Mix meat,1 1/3 cups of the instant puffs (dry), the egg, salt pepper, onion, catsup and milk.

    Spread in ungreased pie pan, 9×1 1/2 inches.

    Bake uncovered 35 to 40 minutes.

  2. Prepare remaining instant puffs as directed on package for 4 servings.

    Top baked meat loaf with mashed potatoes; sprinkle with cheese.

    Bake 3 to 4 minutes longer or until cheese melts. 4 or 5 servings.

  3. Storing, Freezing, and Thawing Uncooked Ground Beef

    STORE, loosely covered, in coldest part of the refrigerator up to 24 hours (if you are planning to use the ground beef within 24 hours of purchasing).

  4. FREEZE for longer storage. Wrap one-pound packs or individual patties in freezer wrap, separating patties with double layer of freezer wrap.

    Label with date. Store no longer than 4 months.

  5. Put a spoonful of stuffing on each piece of cabbage sheet and roll the cabbage. Then, with your finger, push the ends of it inward.

  6. THAW wrapped 5 to 8 hours in refrigerator or cook patties frozen.
